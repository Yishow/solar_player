# Shared Style Recipe

Recipe id: `shared.display-wall-style.v1`

Use this as the base style block for display page photo and visual asset planning.

## Shared visual style

Warm ivory paper surface, corporate green-energy display wall, clean Taiwanese factory sustainability context, soft natural light, low saturation, restrained premium presentation, deep green primary accents, misty green secondary accents, subtle solar gold highlights, quiet botanical ornament language, high readability from distance, polished enterprise presentation, realistic materials, and low visual clutter.

## Photo guidance

- Use realistic or real-photo-like composition.
- Keep the tone warm, clean, and compatible with the ivory display shell.
- Leave calm areas where hero copy or fade overlays may sit.
- Make the subject understandable within three seconds.
- Avoid harsh blue/purple/neon casts, dark moody lighting, excessive blur, over-cropped views, and generic stock-photo feeling.
- Avoid baked-in UI text, labels, numbers, brand marks, signage artifacts, or other details that should be rendered by the app instead.

## Icon guidance

- Final production icons should be SVG.
- Use one visual grammar per set.
- Keep line weight, corner radius, and level of detail consistent.
- Prefer `currentColor` in final SVG so page CSS controls color.
- Avoid 3D, sticker style, heavy shadows, multi-color illustrations, and decorative gradients.

## Ornament guidance

- Ornament assets are decorative only.
- Use low opacity and token-driven green/gold color roles.
- Keep leaf, branch, and gold-line treatments quiet enough that titles, cards, diagrams, and charts remain dominant.

## Avoid list

Avoid generated text, fake marks, UI screenshots, chart labels, close-up identity details, unreadable signs, watermark-like artifacts, oversaturated colors, science-fiction styling, excessive 3D rendering, heavy bokeh, and random decorative glow spots.
